WELCOME TO

PIXEL VAULT

Take back control of your digital photos. Pixel Vault stores your memories securely using blockchain and IPFS—no middlemen, no compromise.

HOW IT WORKS

1.

Connect Your Wallet:

Once the page loads, your wallet will automatically connect. You'll see your Ethereum address under the title "Account".

2.

Connect an Image:

Click the "Choose Image" button to select the photo you want to upload from your device.

3.

Upload to IPFS:

After selecting the image, hit "Upload file". Your photo will be stored securely on the IPFS network and a unique hash (CID) will be generated.

4.

Retrieve Photo by Address:

To access stored photos, enter a valid Ethereum address into the "Enter Address" box and click "Get data."

5.

View the Stored Image:

If that address has uploaded images, they will be retrieved and displayed using the IPFS link associated with them.
